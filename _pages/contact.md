# Contact

Whether you need help with the app, want to suggest a feature, or have spotted a bug, I would love to hear from you. My Vinyls is a solo project, so your message goes directly to the developer, not a faceless support queue.

- Support: [website@myvinyls.app](mailto:website@myvinyls.app)
- Press: [website@myvinyls.app](mailto:website@myvinyls.app)

I aim to respond to every email within 24 to 48 hours, often sooner. If your issue is urgent, such as a sync problem or a crash, mention that in the subject line and I will prioritize it.

When reporting a bug, the most helpful thing you can include is a short description of what you were doing when it happened, what you expected, and what actually occurred. Screenshots are always welcome. If the issue involves Discogs sync, letting me know your Discogs username helps me investigate faster.

Feature requests are just as valuable as bug reports. Many of the features in My Vinyls today started as suggestions from collectors. If there is something you wish the app did, or something that could work better, send it over. Even small ideas often lead to meaningful improvements.

You can also submit and vote on feature ideas on the [My Vinyls feedback board](https://feedback.myvinyls.app). It is the best way to see what other collectors are asking for and to add your voice to requests that matter to you.

For product updates, vinyl tips, and the occasional collection photo, follow along on Instagram. It is the most active place to keep up with what is happening with the app and to connect with other collectors who use My Vinyls.

Thank you for being part of the My Vinyls community.
