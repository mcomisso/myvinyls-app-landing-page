FAQ

Common questions

Is the app really free?

Yes! My Vinyls is free to download and use. Some premium features are available through a one-time purchase - no subscriptions.

Does it work with my Discogs account?

Absolutely. Connect your Discogs account and sync your entire collection in minutes. Changes sync both ways automatically.

Can I use it offline?

Yes! Your entire collection is cached locally. Browse your records anywhere, even without internet.

What about CDs and cassettes?

My Vinyls works with any format in the Discogs database - vinyl, CD, cassette, 8-track, you name it.
