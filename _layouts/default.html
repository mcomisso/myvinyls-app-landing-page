FAQ

Common questions

How does pricing work?

Free to download with core features included. Unlock everything with a one-time purchase or optional subscription. Prefer not to subscribe? A one-time unlock is available.

Does it sync with Discogs?

Yes. Connect your account and sync your entire collection in minutes. Changes sync both ways automatically.

Does it work offline?

Your entire collection is stored locally. Browse and search your records anywhere, even without internet.

What formats are supported?

Everything in the Discogs database — vinyl, CD, cassette, 8-track, and more.
