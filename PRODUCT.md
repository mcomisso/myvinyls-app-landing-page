# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

My Vinyls serves record collectors who want a faster, calmer way to check what they own, add records, browse their library, and understand collection details on iPhone. Website visitors are usually evaluating whether the app fits their collecting habits before downloading it from the App Store.

## Product Purpose

The site explains and sells My Vinyls as a focused vinyl collection manager with Discogs sync, barcode scanning, offline access, marketplace context, and collector-friendly insights. Success means a visitor quickly understands the app, trusts that it is actively maintained, and moves to the App Store with confidence.

## Brand Personality

Warm, precise, collector-minded. The voice should feel practical and personal, with enough polish to match a premium iOS app and enough specificity to avoid generic app-store marketing.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic automatic app landing-page templates, dense release-note dumps, low-contrast gray text, decorative card grids, and SaaS-style metric blocks that do not help collectors decide whether the app is useful.

## Design Principles

- Show the app as maintained and useful, not just attractive.
- Keep collector workflows concrete: owning, searching, scanning, valuing, selling, and rediscovering records.
- Use spacing and hierarchy to make long content scannable without hiding detail.
- Preserve the existing warm brand palette unless a surface has a clear reason to diverge.
- Prefer app screenshots, release details, and real feature language over decorative filler.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast and keyboard-accessible navigation. Keep long-form pages readable on small screens, respect reduced-motion preferences, and avoid using color alone to communicate status or importance.
